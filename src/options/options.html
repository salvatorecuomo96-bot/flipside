Privacy

Nothing is sent when you open a page. Only when you click the FlipSide button, the current article's title, URL, and text are sent to the analysis service — a hosted proxy by default, or directly to Groq if you've added your own key — solely to generate the counter-perspective. No account is required and no personal data is collected. Your optional Groq key is stored only on this device (chrome.storage.local) and is sent only to Groq.
